About Me

Passionate about AI, clean code, and meaningful technology

I'm Marcel, an AI graduate, builder, and creator who enjoys shaping both digital systems and physical forms. My work blends artificial intelligence, clean code, and philosophical thinking, drawing inspiration from Stoicism, Taoism, and the pursuit of elegant simplicity.

I've developed apps, simulations, and tools that solve real problems from a voice-enabled gym tracker to evolutionary game theory models using Unreal Engine. I build with languages like Java, Python, Kotlin, and C#, and I enjoy pushing tech into unexpected places.

Beyond the screen, I train in martial arts, sculpt, and play guitar, disciplines that ground me and sharpen my focus.

For me, tech isn't just about functionality. It's about meaning, precision, and impact.
